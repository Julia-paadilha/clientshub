My rate is $150 per hour — and this is the minimum rate for any tattoo.

Please keep in mind that the time I give you is just an estimate — it may finish a bit earlier or take a little longer. Please be prepared in case it goes beyond the estimated time.

I usually overestimate the time to make sure we have enough space to go through the whole process calmly and comfortably — and to avoid any unexpected extra charges. If your session finishes early, you’ll only pay for the actual time spent.

This includes things like:

  • 🎨 Adjusting your design if needed
  • 📏 Testing different stencil sizes or placements
  • 🎨 Mixing colors (for color tattoos)
  • ⏸️ Taking short breaks if needed
  • 🩹 Wrapping and covering the tattoo properly at the end
