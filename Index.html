Care Instructions

  • Get a full night of sleep before your appointment.
  • Eat a good meal and stay hydrated.
  • Avoid alcohol, caffeine, and blood thinners.
  • Moisturize your skin the week before.
  • Come wearing comfortable clothing.
  • Wash your tattoo gently with antibacterial soap.
  • Apply a thin layer of unscented moisturizer.
  • Avoid scratching, soaking, or sun exposure.
  • Don’t pick scabs — let the skin heal naturally.
  • Keep it clean and let it breathe.
